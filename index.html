Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: October 2025

Acceptance of Terms

By using Techspace Finance services, you agree to be bound by these terms and conditions.

Loan Terms

Loan amounts range from KES 3,500 to KES 35,000 with repayment periods of 61 days. Interest rates are 7% of the loan amount. A service fee is charged and is refundable upon successful completion of your first loan repayment cycle.

Eligibility

You must be:

  • A Kenyan citizen aged 18 years or older
  • Have a valid national ID
  • Own an active M-PESA account
  • Have a verifiable source of income

Repayment

Loans must be repaid in full by the due date. Late payments may incur penalties and affect your credit score.

Service Fees

A service fee is charged upon loan approval. This fee is refundable after your first successful loan repayment.

Default

Failure to repay your loan may result in:

  • Negative reporting to credit reference bureaus
  • Legal action to recover the debt
  • Suspension of future loan applications

Modifications

We reserve the right to modify these terms at any time. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of modified terms.
